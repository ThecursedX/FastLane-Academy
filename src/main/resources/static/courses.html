Frequently asked

Questions we get the most.

Still can't find what you need? Drop us a line.

Do I need to bring my own car?

No — every practical lesson is in a fully-fitted school car (dual controls, current insurance, fresh dashcam). Refresher students may use their own vehicle on request.

What if I miss a session?

You can reschedule up to twelve hours before the lesson start time without paying a fee. Cancellations inside that window are charged at half the lesson rate.

How do payments work?

You can pay the course in full at enrolment, or split it across the six weeks. Both options are visible inside your student dashboard the moment you sign up.

What's the difference between the internal and state exam?

The internal exam is run by one of our own examiners and mirrors the state format. It's free and included. You book and pay the state exam yourself when we tell you you're ready.

Can I switch instructors mid-course?

Yes, no questions asked — we just want to know it isn't working. We'll match you to someone else within forty-eight hours.
