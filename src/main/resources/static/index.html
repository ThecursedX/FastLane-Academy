About us

Our advantage is experience and knowledge.

We opened FastLane in 1992 with a single car and a single classroom. Today we run a fleet of 76 vehicles and one of the highest first-time pass rates in the state — but the lesson plan still starts the same way it always has: a coffee, a calm voice, and a route the student already knows.

Every instructor is an active practitioner. They drive professionally outside of teaching hours, which means what they teach you is what they themselves rely on five days a week.

Refreshers, theory-only courses, and category-specific endorsements — if it touches a wheel, we run a course for it.
